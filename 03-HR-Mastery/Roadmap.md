

# 🎓 HR ROUND MASTERY ROADMAP (Zero → Advanced)

Duration: **8 Weeks**  
Daily Practice: **45–60 mins**

We will build in 5 Levels.

---

# 🟢 LEVEL 0 – Absolute Beginner (Week 1)

### 🎯 Goal: Basic clarity + remove fear

You may:

- Feel nervous
    
- Struggle to speak fluently
    
- Not know how to structure answers
    

That’s normal.

---

## 📅 Week 1 Plan

### Day 1–2: Self Awareness

Write answers to:

- Who am I professionally?
    
- Why did I choose web development?
    
- What are my top 3 skills?
    
- What are my weaknesses?
    

---

### Day 3–4: Introduction Building

Learn simple structure:

```
Present → Skills → Projects → Goal
```

Practice speaking 10 times daily (mirror practice).

---

### Day 5–6: Basic HR Questions

Prepare simple answers for:

- Tell me about yourself
    
- Why should we hire you?
    
- What are your strengths?
    
- What are your weaknesses?
    

---

### Day 7: Record Yourself

Record video.  
Analyze:

- Speed
    
- Confidence
    
- Eye contact
    
- Filler words
    

---

# 🟡 LEVEL 1 – Structured Speaker (Week 2–3)

### 🎯 Goal: Speak clearly and logically

---

## Learn STAR Method

```
S – Situation
T – Task
A – Action
R – Result
```

Use this for:

- Leadership story
    
- Failure story
    
- Conflict story
    
- Achievement story
    

---

## Daily Routine

1. 10 min speaking practice
    
2. 10 min vocabulary improvement
    
3. 15 min HR question practice
    
4. 10 min company research
    

---

# 🟠 LEVEL 2 – Corporate Awareness (Week 4–5)

### 🎯 Goal: Understand corporate world

Learn:

- CTC vs In-hand salary
    
- Notice period
    
- Probation
    
- Bond
    
- Relocation
    
- Salary negotiation basics
    

Prepare answers for:

- Why this company?
    
- Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
    
- Higher studies plan?
    
- Government job preparation?
    

---

# 🔵 LEVEL 3 – Confidence & Psychology (Week 6–7)

### 🎯 Goal: Professional personality

Work on:

- Body language
    
- Eye contact
    
- Hand movement control
    
- Voice modulation
    
- Handling silence
    
- Controlling nervousness
    

Practice:

- 4-4-4 breathing
    
- Slow speaking technique
    
- 2-second pause rule
    

---

# 🔴 LEVEL 4 – Pro HR Candidate (Week 8)

### 🎯 Goal: Handle pressure questions

Prepare for:

- Why gap in resume?
    
- Why low marks?
    
- Why career switch?
    
- Why leaving previous job?
    
- Salary expectation?
    

Also practice:

- Negotiation basics
    
- Smart closing statement
    
- Asking questions to HR
    

---

# 🧪 MOCK INTERVIEW SYSTEM (Very Important)

From Week 3 onward:

Every Saturday:

- Full 20-minute mock interview
    
- Self review
    
- Improve weak answers
    

---
